Idea Formulation

I collaborated on this project with Benjie Fleming and Nathan Judges. The brief stated we had to design an interface for an autonomous vehicles that solves some kind of problem.

We began by researching different use cases and discussed our initial ideas and concepts. We ended up with a few ideas that we thought would be suitable to solve our problem:

1. An autonomous vehicle that transports people interstate. It would be a cheap and efficient alternative to travelling where the individual would be picked up from their homes and taken to their required location using a highway put in place for these vehicles.

2. An small autonomous food delivery vehicle that would deliver from fast food chains to your location

3. An autonomous public transport system with multiple options where you can choose to be picked up sooner and dropped off a little walk away, or wait longer and be dropped off out the front of the location. There would be many of these vehicles around the city that would all communicate with each other to find the best route for the individual.

My favourite was the autonomous interstate travelling vehicle. I like this idea because I think it solves a big problem and there are a lot of people I know, including myself, who could benefit from this. We met up to discuss our favourite ideas and landed on the interstate vehicle.
