Analysis

Creating an affinity diagram was most effective is analysing all of the research I had done. I found four prominent themes across three different kinds of users. I found it interesting that all three users fell into some of the four categories, and that a lot of them have aligning needs. The most common theme was around the lack of access to or the complexity surrounding information and resources.

I noticed that victims were the ones that fell into all four of these categories, and as such, were who I decided to focus on. From this a persona and storyboard was generated to help me empathise with the user before developing any concepts.
